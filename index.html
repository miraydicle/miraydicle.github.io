About Me

I am an electrical engineer with a strong interest in wireless communication systems, signal processing, and the mathematical foundations of communication theory. My work focuses on exploring new ways of understanding and structuring wireless channels, particularly in space and satellite communication environments. I am especially interested in physics-driven approaches that challenge conventional assumptions in communication system design.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Communication Systems
  • OAM & Modal Communications
  • Advanced Signal Processing
  • Satellite & Space Communication Networks
  • Electromagnetic Information Theory
